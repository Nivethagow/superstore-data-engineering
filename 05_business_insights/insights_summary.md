# Business Insights Summary

Derived from four years of Superstore transaction data (2019–2022).

---

## Insight 1: Sales by Category

**Technology is the highest-selling category**, outperforming Furniture and Office Supplies.

| Category | Approx. Total Sales |
|---|---|
| Technology | ~$0.83M |
| Furniture | ~$0.74M |
| Office Supplies | ~$0.71M |

**Takeaway:** Prioritize technology inventory and marketing. Explore cross-sell opportunities pairing tech with office supplies.

---

## Insight 2: Sales by Customer Segment

| Segment | Total Sales | Share |
|---|---|---|
| Consumer | $1.16M | 50.56% |
| Corporate | $0.71M | 30.74% |
| Home Office | $0.43M | 18.70% |

**Takeaway:** The Consumer segment drives the majority of revenue. The Home Office segment, while smallest, may have high growth potential as remote work increases. Corporate accounts warrant targeted B2B outreach given their 30%+ share.

---

## Insight 3: Sales by State

**California leads all states with nearly $2M in sales.** The top states are concentrated in population-dense coastal and Midwest markets.

| Rank | State | Approx. Sales |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | California | ~$1.95M |
| 2 | New York | ~$1.40M |
| 3 | Texas | ~$1.15M |
| ... | ... | ... |
| Last | Arizona | ~$0.28M |

**Takeaway:** Strong West Coast and Northeast performance. Low Arizona sales despite proximity to California — could indicate a market penetration opportunity or distribution gap.
